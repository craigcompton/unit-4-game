Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. It will be between 19 and 120.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you. It is up to you to figure out the value of each crystal when you click on them.

Each time the game starts, the values of the crystals will change to a random number between 1 and 12.
